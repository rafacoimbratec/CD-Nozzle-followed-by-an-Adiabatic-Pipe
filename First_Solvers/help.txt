Guide to use our GUI:

1 - Choose if you want the analytic solver for Quasi-1D Compressible Steady
State Flow in a Nozzle or a pipe.
2 - If you choose the Nozzle case, input the desired pressure ratio (p0/pe),
exit area ratio (Ae/A*) and the gamma (1.4 for air).
3 - Click the Plot button to start plotting the Nozzle drawing (Blue lines)
that will present any shocks present (Red lines) or expansios fans (Green 
lines)
4 - If you wish to see the plot for other values you can click the restart 
button and insert the desired values.
5 - To end the program just click on the "Exit" button.
